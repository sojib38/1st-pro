Welcome to the simple travel blog. Guide to the world!

The idea is to creat a simple, easy-to-read travel blog that can be built upon as I find the time and inspiration.

The reason I started to this blog was to share my experiences, and it has been a great way to connect with other travellers and get feedback from them. I write about all the amazing places that have opened up to me during five years of travelling! This world is full of many beautiful places. But not everyone seems to have enough money to travel. In The Simple Travel, we make travelling easier and budget-friendly for people. Many people think that travelling is only for the rich. Nobody should stop themselves from travelling beacuse of a shortage of money. What makes our blog successful in because we are relatable.

Our primary audience is composed mainly of 21-45 year olds. Full-time employees who wish to take a break from work and explore the world. They are budget-conscious, yes, but are willing to spend hard-earned money on experiences that are worth it.
