ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm N.H., a travel writer, photographer and blogger.


I began travelling solo in 2015 During this time. I have visited more than 25 countries and three continents. I write about amazing places to visit, tasty food to eat and things to do on your next trip You'll also find great jtrips for budget travel and how to save money when travelling.


With every experience, I learn something new about myself and the world around me Travelling taught me humility, compassion, and patience. It has allowed me to meet so many unique individuals from different walks of life that I would have never encountered otherwise.


Travelling has made me appreciate that there are so many ways in which the human body can be beautiful. It has also taught me that you don't have to be wealthly or educated to be kind or wise. There is so much goodness in the world, and I am blessed enough to have been able to see some of it firsthand.


I love sharing my experiences with others so they can see what it's like to travel solo or as a couple/family. I also love writing about destination weddings and honeymoons because they're an amazing way to celebrate your love while exploring a new place together.


I am based in the Shah Alam City area but have lived in Penang, Bintulu and Kuala Lumpur as well, so i know how important it is for people travelling from out of town to find a good hotel at an affordable price.

The best part of travel is meeting like-minded people who are trying to make their own way in this big confusing place we call Earth, but for now, it's just about me and my simple life of just a travel blog.
